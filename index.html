Practice English pronunciation anytime, anywhere — 100% offline.

A comprehensive read-aloud and pronunciation scoring app powered entirely by on-device AI. Practice with 14,829 words; 17,751 dialogue lines (including 800+ common English phrases and idioms) across 109 life-like mini-scenarios in 11 topic areas (travel, campus, work, housing, and more — each with Easy / Medium / Hard and 50 exchanges per level); plus the complete 7-volume McGuffey Eclectic Readers and 10 literary genre collections — all with Chinese translations.

Speech synthesis by Kokoro TTS. Pronunciation scoring by OpenAI Whisper. No internet required. No audio uploaded.

Who Is It For?

  • Learners who want systematic, self-paced English pronunciation practice.
  • Chinese students preparing for CET-4 or CET-6.
  • Exam candidates preparing for IELTS, TOEFL, or other speaking tests.
  • Professionals looking to sharpen conversational English for work scenarios.
  • Parents and students seeking a structured reading curriculum (McGuffey Readers).
  • Anyone who prefers private, offline practice without the pressure of live tutoring.
