SoundsRight 耳顺英语 · Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026 (updates will be noted on this page).

SoundsRight (耳顺英语, "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

100% On-Device Processing

All text-to-speech (TTS) and speech recognition (ASR) run entirely on your device. Your voice is never uploaded. There are no remote servers for speech processing. No internet connection is required for core features.

1. What Stays on Your Device

  • Voice Recordings — When you practice pronunciation, the App records your voice for on-device speech recognition and scoring. Recordings are processed locally and never leave your device.
  • Learning Progress & Settings — Your progress, preferences, and app data are stored locally on your device.

2. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not upload your voice recordings or any other audio to any server.
  • We do not use remote TTS or ASR services.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3. Subscription & Payment

If you purchase a Pro subscription, payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We may receive subscription status (e.g., whether a purchase is active) to unlock Pro features. Payment data is governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

4. Advertising

The App does not use third-party advertisements. We do not use your information for advertising.

5. Data Retention

Your recordings and learning data remain on your device. We do not retain copies. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App.

6. Your Rights

You can disable microphone access in your device settings to stop recording, or uninstall the App to remove all data. Subscription refunds and cancellations are governed by Apple's and Google's respective terms.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly address it.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with the new effective date noted. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please visit our Support & Contact page.
